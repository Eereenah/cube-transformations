The following are the key bindings:

Rotation:
	Around Y axis:
		Clockwise: 	  T
		Counterclockwise: R
	Around X axis:
		Clockwise: 	  E
		Counterclockwise: W
	Around Z axis:
		Clockwise:        Y
		Counterclockwise: U

Camera translation:
	X axis:
		Right: 		  S
		Left: 		  A
	Y axis:
		Up: 		  F
		Down: 		  D		
	Z axis:
		Back: 		  G
		Front: 		  H

FOV:
	Add angle: 		  X
	Subtract angle:   	  Z
